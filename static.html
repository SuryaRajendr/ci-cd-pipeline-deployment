Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta, diced
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, freshly ground
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
How to Make:

1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.

2. In a separate pan, heat olive oil and cook pancetta until crispy.

3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and cheese together. Season with salt and pepper.

4. Drain the spaghetti and mix it with pancetta in the pan.

5. Remove from heat, then quickly toss with the egg mixture to create a creamy sauce.

6. Serve hot, topped with extra cheese and a sprinkle of black pepper.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 200g Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 2 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Cumin powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 tbsp Tomato paste
  • 400ml Coconut milk
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, to garnish
How to Make:

1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, paprika, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and salt. Add chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour.

2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown. Add garlic and ginger, sauté for another minute.

3. Add tomato paste and cook for a few minutes. Stir in coconut milk and bring to a simmer.

4. Add chicken pieces and cook until done, about 10-12 minutes.

5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve with rice or naan.

Lemon Glazed Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 Salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 1 Garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp Chopped parsley, for garnish
  • Steamed asparagus or broccoli, to serve
How to Make:

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, honey, garlic, salt, and pepper.

3. Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush each fillet with the lemon-honey mixture.

4. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.

5. Serve hot with steamed vegetables and garnish with chopped parsley.
